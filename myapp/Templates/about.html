About Us

Welcome to Local Worker Finder. We are a simple platform created to help local people find skilled workers easily.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to connect local workers like plumbers, electricians, and carpenters directly with customers. We want to make it easy for everyone to get help for their daily needs without any confusion.

How to Use This Site

  • Step 1: Select a worker category and your city.
  • Step 2: View the list of available workers and their details.
  • Step 3: Send a work request to the worker you like.
  • Step 4: Once the worker accepts, you can confirm the booking.
  • Step 5: After the work is done, you can give feedback and ratings.

Key Features

  • Easy search for workers by category.
  • Direct booking and status tracking.
  • Rating and review system for trust.
  • Admin panel for overall management.
